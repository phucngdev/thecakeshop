The Cake Bake Shop by Gwendolyn Rogers, Broad Ripple Village

Transport yourself into a magical fairytale and prepare for an unforgettable experience. Whether you are looking for a place to celebrate your birthday, to have an elegant lunch, or romantic dinner our beautifully decorated cake shop is the perfect place. Delicious three-layer cakes, as well as cheesecakes, pies, cookies, and other desserts fill our elegantly designed display case and are served daily. For the savory side, we offer an expanded lunch and dinner menu featuring some traditional dishes as well as our signature quiche and chicken salad. Our menu changes seasonally, to ensure every experience is a new one. Everything on our menu is house made from scratch daily with our commitment to the highest quality and ingredients. Gwendolyn's Cake Bake Shop has become the destination of Indianapolis for celebrating birthdays, weddings, anniversaries and all life's special occasions.

Afternoon Tea is a beautiful tradition that began in nineteenth century England. The Cake Bake Shop honors this tradition by providing our guests with a one of a kind experience. Dine in our beautiful shop, sipping tea, eating finger sandwiches, decadent desserts and feel like royalty. Whether celebrating a special event such as a birthday, engagement, or anniversary, we graciously welcome you to Afternoon Tea.

Tea is served Monday – Friday from 10:00 A.M. – 4:00 P.M. To make a reservation please call the shop directly (317) 257-2253 Ext. 3 or request a reservation on our private event form below. Please allow 48 hours notice for all Afternoon Tea Reservations.

Afternoon Tea reservations are not available on Open Table.

Afternoon Tea is booked as a private event and gift cards are not redeemable for this experience.
